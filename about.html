Our Journey

Founded in 1995 by coffee enthusiast Maria Rodriguez, Brew & Bean began as a small family roastery with a simple mission: to bring the world's finest coffee directly to your cup.

Today, we work directly with farmers across Central and South America, Africa, and Asia to source the highest quality beans while ensuring fair trade practices.
